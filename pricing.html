Extend finperiti with modular add-ons

Add only the capabilities you need

finperiti is designed as a modular compliance platform. Start with the core package and extend functionality with specialized compliance, security, and automation tools.

Global Screening

Automated sanctions, PEP, and watchlist screening directly within finperiti. Real-time alerts integrate into onboarding and ongoing monitoring workflows.

Best forAccounting firms, legal practices, and corporate service providers needing continuous AML screening.

Binarii Labs Security Layer

Sovereign-first, zero-trust data security. Patented encryption, fragmentation, and multi-location storage mean no complete file is ever stored in one place — with blockchain-based Proof of Record for immutable auditability.

Best forRegulated firms and enterprises needing secure document exchange and verifiable audit trails for NIS2, DORA, GDPR, and governance requirements.

Professional Office

Advanced screening — enhanced due diligence with extended datasets, adverse media monitoring, and customizable screening thresholds.

Best forAdvisory, tax, and fintech firms managing higher-risk clients.

ID Verification & Liveness

Automated identity verification with facial liveness detection for secure remote onboarding. Clients can verify identity from any device.

Best forFirms conducting remote client onboarding.

E-Signature

Integrated electronic signature workflows for onboarding forms, declarations, and approvals. Documents remain securely stored within your Microsoft environment and fully linked to AML records.

Best forAlmost all firms — the most commonly selected add-on.

Agentic AI Coming soon

finperiti’s AI-powered compliance assistant. Automates routine reviews, flags anomalies, and drafts risk assessments while learning from your firm’s compliance patterns.

Best forFirms looking to scale compliance operations without increasing headcount.
