Finperiti is designed as a modular compliance platform. Start with the core package and extend functionality with specialized compliance, security, and automation tools.

Global Screening

Automated sanctions, PEP, and watchlist screening directly within Finperiti. Real-time alerts integrate into onboarding and ongoing monitoring workflows.

Best for

Accounting firms, legal practices, and corporate service providers needing continuous AML screening.

Binarii Labs Security Layer

Add sovereign-first, zero-trust data security to your Finperiti environment with Binarii Labs. Secure sensitive files using patented encryption, fragmentation, and multi-location storage technology that ensures no complete file is ever stored in one place.

Seamlessly integrated with Microsoft environments and workflows, Binarii Labs enhances compliance, operational resilience, secure collaboration, and immutable auditability through blockchain-based Proof of Record - all delivered as a modular add-on within the Finperiti platform.

Best for

Financial services, regulated firms, compliance-driven organisations, and enterprises requiring secure document exchange, resilient data protection, and verifiable audit trails for NIS2, DORA, GDPR, and governance requirements.

Professional Office (Advanced Screening)

Enhanced due diligence with extended datasets, adverse media monitoring, and customizable screening thresholds.

Best for

Advisory, tax, and fintech firms managing higher-risk clients.

ID Verification & Liveness

Automated identity verification with facial liveness detection for secure remote onboarding. Clients can verify identity from any device.

Best for

Firms conducting remote client onboarding.

E-Signature

Integrated electronic signature workflows for onboarding forms, declarations, and approvals. Documents remain securely stored within your Microsoft environment and fully linked to AML records.

Best for

Almost all firms — the most commonly selected add-on.

Agentic AI (Coming Soon)

Finperiti’s AI-powered compliance assistant. Automates routine reviews, flags anomalies, and drafts risk assessments while learning from your firm’s compliance patterns.

Best for

Firms looking to scale compliance operations without increasing headcount.
