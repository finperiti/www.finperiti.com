About Us

Smarter Compliance. Less Chaos.

Finperiti was founded in Dublin in 2024 with one goal: make compliance simple again. We were tired of endless email threads, lost documents, and the slow grind of traditional KYC. So, we built a smarter way to do it.
‍
Finperiti takes care of onboarding, document collection, and compliance automatically—perfect for accountants, legal teams, and anyone who deals with secure documentation every day.
‍
All you need is a Microsoft 365 subscription, and Finperiti handles the rest. It’s fast, secure, and built to adapt to the way your business works.
‍
We’re a small, expert team who believe that great technology should make life easier. With Finperiti, compliance isn’t a burden—it’s a breeze.
