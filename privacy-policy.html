Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Welcome to finperiti (“we”, “us”, or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you access or use the finperiti website, products, services, or applications (collectively, the “Services”). By accessing or using our Services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

A. Personal Information

Information you provide directly, such as:

  • Name, email address, phone number, company name, job title
  • Account registration details
  • Customer onboarding data (e.g., customer names, IDs, contact info)
  • Government-issued ID information for compliance purposes

B. Usage Information

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address
  • Log data and interaction history
  • Performance and analytics data

C. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance your experience, remember preferences, and analyze usage. These may include session cookies, analytics cookies, and functional cookies. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Register and manage your account
  • Authenticate and authorize access
  • Process inquiries, support requests, and communications
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • Analyze usage trends and performance
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Send important updates or notifications

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable under data protection laws (such as GDPR), we may process data based on:

  • Your consent (e.g., for cookies)
  • Performance of a contract (e.g., account setup and onboarding)
  • Legal obligations
  • Our legitimate business interests (e.g., security, analytics)

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal information with:

  • Service Providers: Partners that support us (e.g., hosting, analytics, security, customer support)
  • Compliance and Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect legal rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, sale, or acquisition
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize such sharing

We require all partners to follow applicable privacy and security standards.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide and improve our Services
  • Fulfil contractual obligations
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, or tax requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Deleted account data may remain in backup archives for a reasonable period as required by law.

6. Security of Your Information

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information. This includes encryption, access controls, and secure server infrastructure. However, no system is 100% secure — we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent
  • Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below. We will evaluate and respond in accordance with applicable law.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to maintain sessions and login states, analyze usage and performance, and personalize your experience. You can restrict or block cookies through your browser settings, though doing so may affect site functionality.

9. Third-Party Links and Services

Our Services may include links to third-party websites or services that are not controlled by finperiti. These third parties have their own privacy policies — please review them separately.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are intended for adults and businesses. We do not knowingly collect or process personal information of children under the age of 16. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, and processed in, countries other than your own. By using the Services, you consent to such transfers where permitted by applicable law.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will revise the “Last Updated” date. Continued use of our Services after changes indicates acceptance of the updated terms.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests relating to this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact: team@finperiti.com
